This is a game that I recently developed, inspired by basketball. There was less emphasis on the design aspect of the project, as most of the user interface elements were sourced from the Unity Asset Store. As a result, my primary focus was on the development side of the game, including implementing gameplay mechanics, functionality, and overall game systems.

This is the first 2D game that I developed and designed. My design work involved creating and illustrating the entire apartment environment, along with all the objects and components within it. I also designed the characters and developed the game's concept, which was inspired by the TV show *Psych*. The game follows a private investigator with a sixth sense who uses their abilities to solve cases. Finally, I contributed to the design of the game's user interface, creating several of its visual elements.
